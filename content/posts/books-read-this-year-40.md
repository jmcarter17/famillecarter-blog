By Orson Scott Card (22)

- Ender's Game

- Ender's Shadow series (4)

By miscellaneous authors

- Do Androids dream of electric sheep, by Philip K. Dick
- The Ring of Solomon, by Jonathan Stroud

It was a very good year in terms of reading. Marjorie might have felt I spent a little too much time on it, but when you start reading, you can't stop. I'm so glad and grateful for books ... good books. Of course, one cannot neglect the most important ones: scriptures. Well, I did read the Book of Mormon once during the year and the children's version twice with Zeke. I'm also almost done with D&C, so at least, I'm not slacking off on spiritual reading.
